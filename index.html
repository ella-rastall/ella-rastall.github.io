2023 École du Cirque Jules Vernes

This work is based on my experience when visiting the circus school of Amiens. I focused on the women performing on silk acrobatics. The material wrapping round them was beautiful. I was in admiration of everyone at the school and expressed it with a quote from The Tempest by William Shakespeare.

(12 pages 18 X 26 cm)

2023 Ö lait (logo design)

From the name of the street, la fosse au lait, I imagined a milk franchise: “Ö lait”. For the logotype I chose to link the cow and the glass of milk, through simple but impactful shapes that lead to the essential. The Cooper Black font brings a playful and accessible dimension.

2023 Crescendo

A micro-edition named “Crescendo” is a collection of fonts from various books. The name was chosen to explain the progress through the book: it goes from thin fonts to thick fonts. The progression is very subtle hence crescendo.

(120 pages 14.8 X 21 cm)

2022 Graphic Designer Definition

Collaboration with someone who is Korean. We came up with a definition of a graphic designer in French. Given that French is neither of our mother tongues, we worked together the three languages to come up with a definition then print it using risography.

(29.7 X 42 cm)

2022 Alice Project Prototype

This is a prototype that I worked on two other people for the Alice Project. It is a German and Austrian campaign that wishes to bring awareness towards animal cruelty. We were provided with texts written and had to illustrate the stories and cover. Spacing in the book was very important because there was a lot of text and full page drawings.

(48 pages 22 X 33 cm)

2022 Brandt Rhapsodie

As the song says: “I think there’s half a pizza left somewhere but check the date on the box”

I represent the difference and separation of the couple. All that remains is the box, grease, crumbs and the marks of where the pizza used to be. Listening to the song, it sounds like the remains of a couple and their love. The text messages can be compared to the pizza crumbs left in the box.

(33 X 33 cm)
